Frequently asked questions

How does placement work? +

Complete the online level test and a live speaking check. Our academic team confirms your level within 24 h and proposes the best cohort with available seats.

Can I pause or transfer my subscription? +

Yes. You can pause for up to 4 weeks or transfer to a different cohort with 48 h notice. Individual packs can be reassigned once during their validity.

What materials do you use? +

Kids learn with Super Minds and Cambridge Storyfun. Teens and adults follow Open World or Speakout, complemented with digital practice and Cambridge Practice Tests.

Are Cambridge exam fees included? +

Course fees cover preparation, mocks, and assessor feedback. Official Cambridge exam registration fees are paid separately through authorised centres.

How do I access speaking clubs? +

Speaking club sessions are drop-in friendly. Subscribe online, choose your slots, and receive reminders with the weekly topics and vocabulary primer.
