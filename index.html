Frequently asked questions

Transparent answers about schedules, make-ups, certificates, and billing.

How many learners are in a Bridgeway group? +

Group classes run with 6‚Äì10 learners to maximise speaking time. When a cohort is full, we open a waitlist and propose alternative slots before billing.

Can I switch schedules after I enroll? +

Yes. Learners can request a schedule change once per cycle subject to seat availability. Transfers between kids and teens groups include an advisor call.

Do you prepare learners for Cambridge exams? +

Every Cambridge program includes mock exams, speaking workshops, and writing feedback using official rubrics. We also assist with registration at authorised centres in Moldova and Romania.

What happens if I miss a class? +

Group learners receive the class recording, homework brief, and optional catch-up clinic. Individual lessons can be rescheduled with 12-hour notice at no additional cost.

How does billing work? +

Tuition is charged in MDL via card or bank transfer. Speaking Club renews monthly and can be paused anytime. Businesses receive consolidated invoices with cost-centre breakdowns.
