FAQ

+

Answers before you enroll

+
+
+
+ + How are groups formed? + + + +

We use level-test results, age, and learning goals to balance cohorts. Groups stay between six and ten learners for collaborative dynamics.

+
+
+ + Can I pause or transfer? + + + +

Yes. You can pause for up to two weeks or transfer to another schedule if seats are available. Individual packs can be rescheduled with 12-hour notice.

+
+
+ + What materials are included? + + + +

Kids work with Cambridge Super Minds, teens and adults use Open World or Speakout, and exam groups receive dedicated Cambridge practice kits with mocks.

+
+
+ + Do you offer certificates? + + + +

All graduates receive a Bridgeway progress statement. Cambridge candidates also receive exam registration guidance and mock feedback reports.

+
+
+ + How do payments work? + + + +

Pay securely online in MDL. Installments are available for courses longer than eight weeks. Speaking club subscriptions renew monthly and can be canceled anytime.
